# #59 CSV to Web Tables | Fridge Combo

A hyper-minimalist, browser-based tool to instantly convert CSV or Excel-copied data into clean **Markdown tables** or **JSON arrays**. Designed for developers, technical writers, and content managers who need a "zero-friction" workflow.

## 🟢 One Action, Zero Friction
- **Paste & Done:** No file uploads, no "Convert" buttons. Just paste your data.
- **Auto-Cleaning:** Automatically trims extra spaces and removes redundant quotes (`"`) that often clutter Excel-copied text.
- **Smart Detection:** Supports Comma (`,`), Tab (`\t`), and Semicolon (`;`) delimiters automatically.
- **Dual Output:** Toggle between Markdown (for GitHub/Notion) and JSON (for dev environments) with a single tap.

## 🛠 Target Users
- **Technical Writers:** Quickly turn spreadsheets into Markdown for documentation.
- **Developers:** Transform CSV lists into JSON objects for mockups or configurations.
- **Web Admins:** Format pricing or feature tables for blogs and CMS without manual coding.

## 📐 Fridge Combo Design System
- **Background:** `#fff`
- **Primary Color:** `#333`
- **Border Radius:** `15px`
- **Performance:** Optimized for low-spec mobile/PC. 100% browser-only.
- **Privacy:** No login, No tracking, No ads. Your data never leaves your device.

## 📱 Installation (PWA)
This tool is designed to feel like a native app:
1. Open the tool in your mobile browser.
2. Tap "Add to Home Screen".
3. Access your workspace with one tap from your home screen.
